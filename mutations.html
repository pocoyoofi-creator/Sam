Mutations in Steal a Mango (S.A.M)

In S.A.M, mutations play a key role in the players' progress, directly affecting the "luck" and the earnings obtained during the game...

Luck-Based Mutations

Luck-based mutations are random events that occur unpredictably and provide significant multipliers for your earnings...

  • Gold: Multiplies your earnings by x2.
  • Diamond: Multiplies your earnings by x5.
  • Rainbow: Multiplies your earnings by x10.

Admin Abuse Mutations

Admin abuse mutations are more elaborate events, usually with greater prior notice...

Impact of Mutations

Regardless of whether the mutation is luck-based or admin abuse, all of them multiply your luck...
